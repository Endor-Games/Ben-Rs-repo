1.	 Commercial Choices
1.1.	Bizarrely the PS1 almost didn’t happen. “It was the result of a cancelled collaboration between Sony and Nintendo ...” (Petite, 2020).  It’s hard to imagine what the games industry would have looked like today if the collaboration had resulted in a joint project.  Something Nintendo must have regretted given the success of the PlayStation series of consoles.  Although in some ways it expanded the market, leading Nintendo to become more innovative and in essence benefitting consumers by giving them more choices.
